Pepporoni Pizza

MORE CHEESE PLEASE

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 Cup pizza sauce
  • 20 slices of Pepporoni
  • 12 ounces of mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F
  2. Roll out the dough
  3. Add the toppings
  4. Cook the Pizza
  5. Slice and Serve
