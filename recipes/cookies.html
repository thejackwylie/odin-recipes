Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Oatmeal is better than chocolate chip!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 and 1/2 Cups of Oats
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Cup Raisons

Instructions

  1. MIX IT ALL TOGETHER AND MAKE COOKIES
